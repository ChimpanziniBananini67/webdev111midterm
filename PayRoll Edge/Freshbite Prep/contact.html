Quick Answers

How do I pause my delivery for vacation? +
You can pause your subscription directly from your "Plans" dashboard. Simply toggle the status to "Paused" before Thursday at midnight for the following week's delivery.
Are the containers recyclable? +
Yes! All Freshbite Prep containers are made from 100% BPA-free, recyclable plastic and our outer insulation is biodegradable cornstarch-based foam.
Can I customize my macros? +
Absolutely. While our standard plans follow Keto/Paleo/Vegan guidelines, you can add "Special Instructions" to any meal in your Weekly Menu to request low-sodium or specific protein swaps.
