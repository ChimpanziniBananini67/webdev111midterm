Our Culinary Philosophy

Freshbite Prep was founded in 2021 when a clinical nutritionist and a Michelin-trained chef realized the "healthy" meal prep industry was failing on flavor and transparency.

We believe food is medicine, but it should also be a celebration. By removing the friction of grocery shopping, chopping, and macro-calculating, we give you back 10+ hours a week to focus on what matters—your performance and your family.
